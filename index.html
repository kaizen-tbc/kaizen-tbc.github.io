Bench Rotation
Class-locked rotation, ranked by attendance consistency

Rotation is typically class-based, not role-based, because raid comp and class utility matter. We rotate where we have more raiders than slots in the exact same class, so raid comp is never affected, only the surplus. We want this to be fair and transparent for the classes we're heavily stacked on, but attendance, performance, and comp can also factor into the decision. If you're on the bench, just confirm you're ready to fill in on raid night. It's a rotation, and your turn comes around.

Tanks Don't Rotate
Tanks aren't part of the bench. We plan around tank absences with an off-spec sub or a pugged tank if needed.
No Sign Up, No Slot
Confirming each week is what claims your spot. Show up ready, and your turn on the bench comes back around.
Rotation Is Tracked
Officers track who's rotated through bench and for how long, so everyone gets fair time in the raid.

Only a few classes are tracked here right now, wherever we're currently stacked. That list grows as recruiting shifts.
